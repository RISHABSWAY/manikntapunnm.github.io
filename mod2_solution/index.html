Best Honda Bikes of all time.

Honda Shine

The Honda Shine is a commuter bike offered at a starting price of Rs. 77,338 in India. There are 2 variations and 5 colors, the price of the best variation starts from Rs. 81,506. The Honda Shine is powered by a 124cc BS6 engine that develops 10.59 HP and 11 Nm of torque. With drum brakes on the front and rear, Honda Shine has devised a compound braking system for both wheels. This shine bike weighs 114 kg and has a tank capacity of 10.5 liters.

Honda Unicorn

The Honda Unicorn is a street bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1.03.992 in India. Available in 1 var and 3 colors. The Honda Unicorn is powered by a 162.7 cc BS6 engine that develops 12.73 hp and 14 Nm of torque. With front disc brakes and rear drum brakes, the Honda Unicorn features an anti-lock braking system. color options include Pearl Ignius Black, Imperial Red Metallic and Matte Axis Gray Metallic. Unicorn bike weighs 140 kg and tank capacity of 13 lits.

Honda Livo

The Honda Livo is a commuter bike that comes at a starting price of Rs. 75,231 in India. There are 2 variations and 4 colors, the price of the best variation starts from Rs. 79,276. The Honda Livo is powered by a 109.51cc BS6 engine that develops 8.67 hp and 9.30 Nm of torque. With drum brakes on the front and rear wheels, the Honda Livo offers a combined braking system for both wheels. This Livo bike weighs 115 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 9 liters.
